ABOUT

Welcome to my website! I'm Wilbur Shi, a sophomore studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at UC Berkeley. Since I was young, I've been fascinated with technology - from setting up my family's old desktop as child to trying out a new VR game, the instruction manuals were never enough to read. I usually tend to look more for the why, and that's why you'd probably see me on Wikipedia or watching some YouTube explanation videos of a concept I learned in class. I have a passion for programming and building things with a tangible impact. I just published two Android apps to the Google Play Store and am currently working on a Chrome Extension as a personal side project.
